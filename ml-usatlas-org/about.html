About

The CODAS computational platform is the result of a collaborative effort between the University of Chicago and the Unversity of California, San Diego. It leverages software and infrastructure from the ATLAS Midwest Tier2 Center, the Pacific Research Platform, the CHASE-CI project,the SLATE project, Virtual Clusters for Community Computation (VC3) and the Open Science Grid.
